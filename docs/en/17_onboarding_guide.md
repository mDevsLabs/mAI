# Onboarding Guide 🚀

Welcome to **mAI**! This guide is designed to take new users step-by-step through their very first steps in the application.

## Step 1: May's Welcome Message

When launching the application for the first time, **May**, your default virtual assistant, will open automatically. She will guide you through the first configuration steps and answer any immediate questions you have about the app.

## Step 2: Configuring API Keys

For mAI to function, you need to configure at least one language model provider:
1. Click the configuration button prompted by May or open the settings (`Ctrl + ,`).
2. Select the **Language Models** tab.
3. Enter your API key for the provider of your choice (OpenAI, Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini) or enable a local provider like Ollama.
4. Save to secure your key locally on your device.

## Step 3: Your First Conversation

1. Click on **New Chat** in the left sidebar.
2. Type your message in the text area at the bottom of the screen.
3. Press **Enter** to launch your first conversation with May!

## Step 4: Exploring and Creating Agents

Go to the **Agents** tab to explore preconfigured profiles or click on **Create Agent** to design a virtual companion tailored to your specific needs.
