# Introduction to mAI 🚀

Welcome to the official documentation for **mAI**, the next-generation artificial intelligence application designed to maximize your productivity and simplify your interactions with Large Language Models (LLMs).

## What is mAI?

**mAI** is a modern, fast, and fully customizable chat framework and conversational agent platform. It allows users to centralize their access to multiple AI providers (such as OpenAI GPT, Claude, Gemini, or local models via Ollama) within a sleek and fluid user interface.

## May: Your Core Assistant 🤖

At the heart of mAI is **May** (formerly Lobe AI), your default intelligent assistant. Designed to be highly versatile, May assists you from the very first launch to guide you, answer complex questions, help with coding, or structure your thoughts. May adapts to your needs using persistent memory and her capability to use external tools (plugins).

## Why Choose mAI?

- **Multi-model support**: Switch instantly between different AI providers.
- **Privacy-first**: Your API keys and chat histories are stored securely locally or on your own infrastructure.
- **Feature-rich**: Integrated screen capture, visual chat sharing, plugin support, and much more.
