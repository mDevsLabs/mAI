# May: Core Assistant 🤖

**May** is the default assistant at the heart of the **mAI** experience. More than just a chat agent, she acts as a guide and a bridge between you and the system's powerful features.

## Role and Capabilities of May

May is designed to be your daily helper. Here are her main roles:
- **Welcoming and Onboarding**: She helps you configure your first API keys and discover the user interface.
- **Technical and General Support**: She answers questions about the application itself or any topic in general culture, science, art, and technology.
- **Development Assistance**: She understands and writes code in multiple programming languages.
- **Plugin Integration**: By default, May has access to enabled plugins such as web browsing or the artifacts system to enrich her answers.

## Customizing May

Although May is the base assistant, you can modify her behavior in her settings:
- Adjust her base system prompt.
- Change her default language model.
- Enable or disable specific plugins for her chat session.
