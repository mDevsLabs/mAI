# mAI 🚀

mAI organizes your agents for 24/7 operations. It recruits, schedules, and generates reports for your entire AI team. You remain in control — without needing to stay online.

**May** is your core AI assistant, ready to help you with all your tasks.

## Key Features

mAI is a workspace to find, create, and collaborate with agents that evolve alongside you. In mAI, we treat agents as the primary unit of work, offering an infrastructure where humans and agents co-evolve.

### 👥 Agents as a Unit of Work
Schedule and monitor the activity of your entire AI team.
- **Increased Productivity**: Group all your agents under one roof.
- **Integrated Messaging**: Chat with your agents directly from your favorite applications.

### 🛠️ Agent Builder
Building a customized AI team starts with the Agent Builder. Describe your needs once, and the agent auto-configures itself for instant use.
- **Unified Intelligence**: Seamlessly access all models and modalities under your control.
- **Expanded Skills**: Connect your agents to your daily tools using compatible plugins and MCP servers.

### 🤝 Collaboration
mAI introduces Agent Groups, allowing you to work with agents as if they were real colleagues. The system assembles the right agents for the task, enabling parallel collaboration and continuous improvement.
- **Pages**: Draft and refine documents with multiple agents at the same time using a shared context.
- **Scheduling**: Schedule runs and let agents work at the right time, even when you are offline.
- **Projects**: Organize work by projects to keep a clear structure.

### 🧠 Human-Agent Co-evolution
The best AI is the one that understands you deeply. mAI features a personal memory that enriches itself over your interactions.
- **Continuous Learning**: Your agents learn from the way you work.
- **Transparent Memory**: Your agents use a structured, editable memory that you control entirely.
